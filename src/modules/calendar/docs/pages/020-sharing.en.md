# Invites and ICS

## How to invite

You invite people to an event by phone number:

1. Open an event or [create a new one](page:overview).
2. In the **Invites** section, type a phone number (for example, +998 90 123 45 67) and add it.
3. Click **Save** — the invite is sent.

> **Privacy:** when you type a number, the owner's name is not shown. The name appears only **after they accept the invite** — and from then on they are recognized the next time the number is typed (they show up in suggestions).

## How to accept an invite

If you are invited, a badge appears on the **bell** (My invites) in the calendar header:

1. Click the bell — the list of invites opens.
2. Choose **Accept** or **Decline**.
3. An accepted event appears in your calendar.

## ICS link — connecting the calendar outward

You can also see the calendar in Apple or Google Calendar:

1. Click the **chain-link** button in the header — the **ICS link** popup opens.
2. Copy the link.
3. Add it to Apple Calendar or Google Calendar as a **subscription** — events keep updating automatically.

> The link is **secret** — anyone who knows it can see your events. Do not share it with strangers.

## Subscribing to an external ICS calendar

It works the other way too — you can add an external calendar (for example, holidays or a schedule from another service):

1. On the left panel, click **+** (New calendar).
2. Choose **ICS subscription** as the type.
3. Enter the ICS link (https://…/calendar.ics) and save — its events appear in your calendar.
