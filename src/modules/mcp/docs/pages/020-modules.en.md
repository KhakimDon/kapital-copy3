# MCP modules

After [connecting](page:connect), the AI can work with the following modules. Each one comes with example prompts — just type them into the chat.

## 📋 Tasks

Sees the board, creates tasks, moves them between columns, assigns people, writes comments.

- "Create 'Prepare the report' in the DEV project and assign Ali"
- "Show my open tasks and tell me which is the most urgent"

## 📚 Wiki

Reads pages, searches them, creates new pages.

- "Find the onboarding guide in the Wiki and summarise it"
- "Create a page called 'New employee checklist'"

## 📅 Calendar

Sees, creates and updates events (including recurring ones); sends invites by phone number — with the same privacy rule: the name behind a number is not shown until the invite is accepted; provides the ICS feed link; sees task due dates.

- "Schedule 'Team sync' tomorrow at 15:00 and invite +998 90 123 45 67"

## 📁 Files

Sees folders, reads text files, searches both by name and **by content**.

- "Find documents that mention the word 'rent'"
- "What's in the contracts folder?"

## 📄 EDO / Didox documents

Document lists, statuses and details; counterparty info by TIN; MXIK code lookup.

- "What's the status of the invoices that arrived this month?"
- "Which company does TIN 302936161 belong to?"

## 🧾 Taxes (Soliq)

Overview, reports, payments, receipts (cheques), rent data.

- "Show the tax payments for June"
- "Are there any unsubmitted reports?"

## 👥 Employees (HR)

Employees, departments, payroll, timesheet, leaves, public holidays.

- "Who is on leave in July?"
- "List the employees of the IT department"

## 🏢 Companies

Company info and a general overview.

- "Give me a short summary of my company"

> The tenant admin can disable any module or switch it to read-only mode under **Settings → MCP**. The AI then cannot change anything in that module.
