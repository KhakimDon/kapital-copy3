# Notifications

The **bell** in the top-right corner shows a counter of new events:

![Notifications](img/notifications.png)

You get notified when: a task is **assigned** to you or taken away, you're added as a **watcher**, a task becomes **urgent**, gets **completed**, edited or commented, you're **mentioned**, or a **due date** is near. Clicking a notification opens the card itself.

> To follow a task: right-click → **Watch**. Every change to it will now reach you too.
