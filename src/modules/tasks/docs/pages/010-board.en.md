# Meet the board

This module is your team's task board (like Jira/Trello). Every piece of work is a **card**, and cards move through **columns** (stages) left to right: *To do → In progress → Review → Done*.

> Open it from the left menu — **Tasks**. Pick a company at the top first — every company has its own board.

![The task board](img/board.png)

What's on screen:

- **Columns** — stages of work. Each column header shows its name, card count and percentage.
- **Cards** — tasks. A card shows: its number (e.g. `DEV-12`), title, the assignee's avatar, a priority marker and labels.
- **The 🔥 icon** — a task with *high* or *urgent* priority is "on fire".
- On top: the **view switcher** (Board / List), **search**, **grouping** and **filters**.
