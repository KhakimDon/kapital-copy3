# Assign someone

1. Open the card, click the **Assignee** field and pick a person.
2. Or faster: **right-click** the card → **Assignee** → pick a person.

The person gets a **notification** immediately. A task can have several assignees.

> The right-click menu is the fastest path: open, priority, assignee, move to column, watch, duplicate and delete — all in one place.
