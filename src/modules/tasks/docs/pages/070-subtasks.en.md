# Subtasks and Epics

- **Subtasks** split a big task into parts: create a checklist of small tasks inside the card; their progress shows on the parent card. When a subtask completes, the parent task's people are notified.
- **An Epic** is a big theme that groups several tasks (e.g. "Mobile app v2"). Epics can link cards across projects; the epic shows as a colored chip on the card.
