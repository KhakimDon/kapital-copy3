# FAQ

- **I can't see my card.** A filter or search may be active; completed cards may have auto-hidden.
- **I can't move a card into a column.** The column has a role restriction — ask the project owner.
- **I didn't get a notification.** You never get notified about your own actions (only others do).
- **I deleted a task.** Deletion is permanent — think twice before deleting anything important.
