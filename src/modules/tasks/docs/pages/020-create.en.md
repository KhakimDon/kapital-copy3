# Create your first task

1. Click **+ Add task** under the column you want.
2. Type a title and press **Enter** — the card appears in that column.
3. Click the card to open it and add details.

![Quick add](img/quick-add.png)

> Make the title a short, clear action: "Fix the login page error" — good; "problem" — bad.

> Tip: you can assign someone right while creating — see [Assign someone](page:assign).
